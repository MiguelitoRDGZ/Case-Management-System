Strategic Changes

Since I stepped into a managerial role, the Brand Management department has undergone a foundational shift in how it operates and contributes to company-wide success. We've transitioned from reactive case handling to strategic, insight-driven improvements.

Deep Complaint Analysis

We've redefined our approach by implementing a deep-dive analysis of all customer complaints and feedback. These insights are now compiled and shared with Operations on a weekly basis to drive systemic improvements.

Agent Performance Tracking

Introduced a performance tracking sheet that allows QA to detect behavioral patterns, flag coaching opportunities, and improve overall agent output—directly enhancing the customer experience.

Social Media Ownership

Fully took over social media support 1,197 social-media-only cases resolved from July 2024 to today.

Insight-Driven Follow-Ups

Customer Success began calling all recently closed claims to gain real insights into the customer's experience. This shift moved us away from purely review collection and toward a richer understanding of pain points, satisfaction levels, and areas for improvement.

Guardian Complaint Oversight

Brand Management took over all Guardian-level customers who no longer qualified for the program but submitted complaints. This proactive handling helped prevent negative reviews and maintained our premium-tier reputation.

Internal Complaint System

We introduced the Internal Complaints process—a completely new initiative that allows us to identify and resolve customer concerns before they escalate to third-party platforms. This has helped reduce external escalations and protect our public reputation.

BBB Ownership & Strategic Response

We fully took over BBB complaint handling from Compliance and introduced a new methodology:

  • Providing detailed, structured breakdowns of each customer case
  • Transparently outlining the steps we took to assist

This has strengthened our credibility and improved resolution rates.

Infrastructure Enhancements

As part of our operational evolution, we've developed and upgraded several internal tools and modules to support scalability, accountability, and performance visibility across all areas of Brand Management.

Enhanced Reviews & Ratings Module

Improved the architecture and reporting logic to better capture, categorize, and measure positive review performance across all platforms.

Complaints Module (Internal Tracking)

Launched a centralized tool to track internal complaints, both organically sourced and manually submitted—giving us better visibility into recurring issues.

Customer Success Module

Built a dedicated module for Customer Success operations, focused on tracking outreach, claim outcomes, and review results from post-claim follow-ups.

FAQ for Review Incentives

Published an internal FAQ to clarify rules, eligibility, and payout structure for the review incentive program—ensuring consistency across departments.

Negative Review Agenda Enhancements

Revamped the existing process with automations, follow-up logic, and clearer case ownership to reduce resolution delays and missed escalations.

Enhanced Reviews & Complaints Sheet

Redesigned our core tracking sheet for both positive reviews and complaints, making it easier to audit outcomes, identify trends, and assess team performance in real time.

Review Insights

2024 Performance

The year started with 1,021 positive reviews in January and showed a dramatic spike in April with 2,427 reviews. However, this spike was due to every review being counted, including those from platforms that do not contribute to our core reputation metrics. It also included updated and edited reviews, technician reviews posted multiple times on the same platform, and other duplicates. As a result, the April surge does not reflect a true organic growth trend but rather an overcounting across several non-impactful sources.

2025 Comparison

Steady Growth: Consistent month-over-month increases (30% average growth rate)

Positive Trajectory: June 2025 surpassed June 2024 by 51% (1,542 vs 1,021)

Catch-up Pattern: While 2025 started lower, our accelerated growth rate and improved data accuracy have positioned us to surpass 2024 totals by Q3 — indicating not just recovery, but stronger and more sustainable performance overall.

Agents Collaborating Towards Reviews: While 2025 started lower with 16 agents participating in the review incentive program in January, we have increased to 32 agents participating in the review incentive program

Negative Review Metrics

2023 Overview:

Sep–Dec 2023: Complaints started fluctuating moderately. Sep 98 received / 37 removed, Oct 124 received / 30 removed, Nov 77 received / 31 removed, Dec 65 received / 35 removed. Removal efficiency ranged from 30–50%.

2024 Highlights:

Early Spike: Jan–Feb saw high complaint numbers: Jan 107 / 57 removed, Feb 99 / 44 removed, showing a 53% Removal rate.

March Peak: Only 90 complaints received, but 79 removals — showcasing an 88% removal efficiency.

Steady Handling: Apr–Jul had consistent handling: 98–145 complaints per month with removal ratios between 40%–65%.

August Surge: Aug received the highest volume (152), with 95 removals — demonstrating strong capacity, showing a 62.5% Removal Rate

End of Year: Oct–Dec removals averaged 70% effectiveness (100/89, 89/62, 114/62).

2025 Trends:

Strong Start: Jan 139 complaints, 122 removed — 87% efficiency.

Consistency Maintained: Feb–Apr hovered above 100 complaints and 60–95 removals.

Improvement Shown: June showed 106 removals out of 152 complaints — indicating above-average capacity even as volume rose, showing a 70% removal rate.

Outlook:

The team demonstrated higher removal capacity during increased volume months, particularly in 2025. The trend points toward not only sustained volume management but improved effectiveness.

Google Reputation Transformation

When I joined, our Google presence had just over 11,000 reviews, and several state-level Google listings were below 4.0 stars. Today, we have reached 15,737 reviews with a 4.7-star rating, and every state rating is now at or above 4.0.

This improvement shows growth in both volume and quality, with over 4,700 new reviews added. It reflects the impact of structured outreach, agent workshops, and consistent follow-up strategies focused on both national and local reputation.

Reddit Engagement

Reddit Engagement Strategy

To shift sentiment and build organic trust, I launched a targeted Reddit engagement project focused on high-traffic homeowner and real estate subreddits.

Scope of Work

  • Engaged across 9+ subreddits including r/homeowners, r/realestate, r/hvacadvice, and r/firsttimehomebuyer
  • Contributed to over 60+ unique Reddit posts and comment threads
  • Included a mix of original posts, helpful comments, upvotes, and strategic replies
  • Responded authentically to home warranty-related discussions, inserting Liberty Home Guard into key decision-making conversations
  • Focused on real user experiences, trust-building language, and SEO-relevant keywords (claims process, repair timelines, specific appliances, etc.)

Strategic Impact

  • Position LHG as a credible, top-rated alternative to larger competitors like AHS and First American
  • Help neutralize negative threads and shift tone in high-visibility posts
  • Boost traffic to branded review pages and increased direct lead interest from forums

BBB Complaint Reduction

BBB Complaint Reduction

Highlights:

From January to June 2024, we received 286 BBB Complaints.

From January to June 2025, that number dropped to 178 BBB Complaints - a 38% reduction

This decline is the direct result of:

  • Preventative handling strategies
  • The implementation of our Internal Complaints process, which allows us to address issues before they escalate externally
